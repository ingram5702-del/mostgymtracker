MostGym Tracker

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MostGym Tracker ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Workout data (exercises, sets, reps, weight, RPE, notes)
  • Workout history and statistics
  • Workout templates you create
  • App preferences and settings

The App also uses the device camera solely for scanning QR codes on gym equipment. Camera images are processed on-device and are never stored or transmitted.

3. How We Use Your Information

All workout data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. We use this data exclusively to:

  • Display your workout history and progress
  • Calculate statistics and track your performance
  • Provide exercise suggestions based on past workouts
  • Manage workout templates

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Firestore — used solely to retrieve remote app configuration. No personal data or workout data is sent to Firebase.
  • Google ML Kit (on-device) — used for QR code scanning. All processing happens locally on your device; no images are sent to any server.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal data or workout information with any third parties. Your data stays on your device.

6. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. We do not have access to your data. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

7. Camera Permission

The App requests camera access only when you use the QR Scanner feature. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time through your device settings. The camera is used exclusively for reading QR codes and no images are captured, stored, or transmitted.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
